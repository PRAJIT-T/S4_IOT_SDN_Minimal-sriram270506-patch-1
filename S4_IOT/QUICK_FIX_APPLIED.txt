WHAT THIS FIX DOES:
  The SO_REUSEADDR option tells the operating system to allow
  immediate reuse of the port, preventing "Address already in use"
  errors when restarting the application.

  Without it: Port is reserved for 60+ seconds after shutdown
  With it: Port is immediately available for reuse
